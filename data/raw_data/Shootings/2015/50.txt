At least four people were injured Friday evening during a shooting in northeast Oklahoma City.  About 9 p.m., police received multiple calls about shots fired at an apartment complex in the 1300 block of N Stiles Avenue. At least four people were shot, and three were taken to a local hospital, police said. One person’s injuries were reported as critical.  The victims’ names have not been released.  Police are investigating the shooting.  Check NewsOK.com for updates.
