Five people were wounded and one killed in a shooting on Friday in Lancaster, police said.  Shortly before midnight, police responded to the 1500 block of Reynolds Street, near West Pleasant Run Road, where they had received a call about shots that were fired from a vehicle into a crowd, police said.  The vehicle fled in an unknown direction.  The six people who were shot were transported to an area hospital where one later died, police said.  Anyone with information about the case is asked to contact Lancaster police at 972-218-2711 or email CID@lancaster-tx.com with tips.  More details to come.
