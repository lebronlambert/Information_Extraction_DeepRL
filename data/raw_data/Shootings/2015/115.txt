BUFFALO, N.Y. (WIVB) – Police are investigating a shooting on Buffalo’s east side that killed an 18-year-old Buffalo man.  Four other people were also struck by gunfire just after 9 p.m. Saturday on Warren near Broadway. Police say the gunfire killed 18-year-old Alonzo Scott.  A 25 year-old Buffalo male is listed in serious condition and a 23 year-old Buffalo female is listed in fair condition at ECMC. Two Buffalo males,19 and 22, were treated and released from ECMC.  Detectives say the shooting appears to be gang related.
