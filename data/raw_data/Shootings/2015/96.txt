Updated with more details at 11:48 a.m.  Four people were shot during an attack Sunday night in the 2700 block of Dayton Street in the Jeff-Vander-Lou neighborhood of St. Louis.  Officers called to the scene found the victims lying in the entrance to an apartment. The victims were taken to an area hospital and are all in stable condition.  Police said that two men, both 25, and two women, ages 25 and 31, were standing outside when someone from a grey vehicle fired shots at them around 10:40 p.m.  Police said the victims gave conflicting accounts of the incident and were uncooperative with investigators.  Children belonging to two of the victims were inside of the apartment at the time of the attack and were unharmed. They are now in the custody of family members, police said.
