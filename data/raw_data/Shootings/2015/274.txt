NOPD: 4 women injured following Lower Ninth Ward shooting Updated: Monday, October 19, 2015 8:10 AM EDT  New Orleans police are investigating a shooting in the Lower Ninth Ward that left four women injured. The incident occurred Sunday around 9 p.m. near the intersection of Choctaw Street and Florida Avenue.  Police say that the four women were brought to a local hospitals. Their injuries are non-life threatening.  Officers initially said three women were injured in the shooting. The fourth victim arrived at a hospital later.  The women were standing on a basketball court when shots were fired, according to the NOPD.
