BUFFALO, N.Y. (WIVB) — Buffalo Police Officers responded Monday evening to a quadruple shooting on Roosevelt Avenue on the city’s east side.  Police arrived to the scene of the serious shooting just before 5:30 p.m. on the first block of Roosevelt Avenue. Police say three were injured, but one 18-year-old man died at the scene.  A 17-year-old male was treated and released from ECMC. Another 17-year-old male remains at ECMC Tuesday morning listed in good condition. The third victim, 19-year-old man, is listed in fair condition at ECMC.  The shooting appears to be gang related, according to Buffalo Police.
