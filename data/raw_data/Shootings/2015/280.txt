Police on the scene Monday night of reported shooting near downtown Evansville. It happened in an apartment attached behind the 711 Tavern on E. Virginia St. just seven blocks of Main St.  Officers confirm five people are being treated at area hospitals for gunshot wounds. Police are trying to talk to some possible suspects and victims.  711 Tavern is still open at this time. Eyewitness News has a crew on scene and is working to get more information.
