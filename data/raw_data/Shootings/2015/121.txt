At least one person is dead and thee others injured after a shooting in Houston's Third Ward.It happened early this morning on Burkett at Berry.Police arrived and found the body of an 18-year-old male at the scene. Two other men and one woman were also shot. They were taken to the hospital in unknown condition.Police are looking into reports that the shooting may be the result of an argument that started at a club. They are also looking into reports that it may be gang related.Anyone who may have witnesses what happened is encouraged to call HPD or Crime Stoppers at 713-222-TIPS.
