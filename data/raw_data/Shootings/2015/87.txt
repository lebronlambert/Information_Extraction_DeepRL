WASHINGTON – One person is dead, and four others injured, after a Tuesday night shooting in Prince George’s County, Maryland.  It happened around 11:15 p.m. along Pard Road and Glacier Avenue in Capitol Heights.  Police say five male victims were taken to the hospital, and one of the victims died. At this time, police have not released any information about the conditions of the other victims.  Detectives are investigating who shot the five victims, and why. They are investigating the shooting as a homicide.  Anyone with information about the shooting should call 1-866-411-TIPS.
