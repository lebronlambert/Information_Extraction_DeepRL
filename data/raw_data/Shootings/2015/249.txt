Danville – Danville police officers are investigating a shooting incident that sent four people to the hospital late Friday night.  Director of Public Safety Larry Thomason said police were called to the 1300 block of May Street around midnight Friday. Officers found two people injured at the scene and learned that two others were transported by private vehicles to the hospital, according to Thomason.  Thomason said that the incident occurred when “young people had gathered for a party and at some point shots were fired.”  “Information has been sketchy, but police continue their investigation following up leads,” Thomason stated in a press release.
