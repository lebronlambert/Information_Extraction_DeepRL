Davenport police are investigating an early morning shooting incident that left five people wounded.  Police officers responded to both Genesis West and East campuses about 5:20 a.m., Friday, in response to multiple subjects with gunshot wounds.  Upon investigation officers located the scene of the shooting in the south alley of is 917 W 3rd St.  A total of five individuals were struck. All wounds are non-life threatening.  The incident is currently being investigated by the Detective Bureau.  If anyone has information reference this incident they are encouraged to call the Davenport Police Department.
