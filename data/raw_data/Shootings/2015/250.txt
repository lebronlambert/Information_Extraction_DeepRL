Police said four people were shot outside a jazz club in the 18th & Vine District early Sunday.  Officers said two groups of people exchanged gunfire outside the Juke House at 18th Street and Highland Avenue just before 3 a.m.  Emergency crews took one person found at the scene to a hospital. A second person flagged down first responders near downtown Kansas City and was taken to a hospital. A third victim went to a hospital in a private vehicle.  A fourth victim was grazed by a bullet.  Investigators said between 50 and 60 shell casings have been found at the scene.  Police said they've been talking to witnesses and examining dashboard camera video from officers who were in the area.  Anyone with information is asked to call the Crime Stoppers TIPS Hotline at 816-474-8477.
