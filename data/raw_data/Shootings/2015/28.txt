Six hit in early morning Crockett County shooting Posted: Sunday, February 8, 2015 10:31 AM EST Updated: Sunday, February 8, 2015 11:10 PM EST  Six people were hit in a Crockett County shooting early Sunday morning.  The shooting happened outside the Malone Bar and Grill in Maury City. According to the Crockett County Sheriff's Office, approximately 30 rounds were fired.  Two victims remain in extremely critical condition. One victim is in critical condition, and the other is stable.  Three people suffered non-critical injuries, and may have been released from the hospital.  Crockett County Sheriff Troy Klyce says they have no suspects in custody, but they are still investigating the situation.  Copyright 2015 WMC Action News 5. All rights reserved.
