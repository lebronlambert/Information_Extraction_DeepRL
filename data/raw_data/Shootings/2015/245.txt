Five injured in Peach St. drive-by shooting Posted: Wednesday, September 23, 2015 11:28 PM EDT Updated: Wednesday, September 23, 2015 11:28 PM EDT  Shreveport police say five people were shot around 7:30 Wednesday (Sept. 23) during a drive-by shooting in the 2900 block of Peace St.  SPD's Breck Scott said in a press release that the suspect's vehicle was described as a "dark colored sedan going east."  The five victims were standing on Knox St. when they were hit by the gunfire. Four people were treated at University Health for non-life threatening injuries. SPD says the fifth victim refused aid from the fire department and fled the scene.
