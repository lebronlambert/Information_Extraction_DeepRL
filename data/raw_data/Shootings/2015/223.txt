Teen shot, killed near Desert Inn and Hollywood identified Posted: Saturday, September 5, 2015 8:39 PM EDT Updated: Saturday, November 14, 2015 8:39 PM EST  The Clark County Coroner's Office identified at 19-year-old who died in a shooting in the east Las Vegas Valley early Saturday morning.  The cause and manner of death for Geovany Garcia, of Las Vegas, remained pending Sunday.  According to Las Vegas Metro police, the shooting was reported just after 2:30 a.m. in the area of Desert Inn Road and Hollywood Boulevard. When officers arrived they found a male in a car with an apparent gunshot wound. He was pronounced dead at the scene.  According to LVMPD's Homicide Section, a big group of people gathered earlier that night at a park in the 5000 block of Hollywood Boulevard. A fight broke out during the gathering and, as people were leaving the area, there was an exchange of gunfire. Four people were hit.  Three victims transported themselves to area hospitals and the fourth was pronounced dead on scene by medical personnel. No arrests have been made in connection with this shooting.  Anyone with information is urged to contact police or remain anonymous by calling Crime Stoppers at 702-385-5555.  This is the 83rd homicide in Metro's jurisdiction for 2015.
