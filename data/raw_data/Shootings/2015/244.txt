Five people are shot in what Shreveport Police say was a drive-by shooting.  It happened just after 7:30pm on the 2900 block of Peach Street.  Shreveport Police say a dark colored sedan was driving east when someone inside fired multiple shots.  No arrests have been made and police have not identified a suspect(s).  All of the victims are expected to be okay.
