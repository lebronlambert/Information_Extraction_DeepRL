NORTH AMITYVILLE, N.Y. (CBSNewYork) — One person was killed and three others were injured during a fight on Long Island early Saturday morning.  It happened around 2 a.m. Saturday when a fight broke out outside the Hollywood Lodge on Coolidge Avenue in North Amityville, 1010 WINS’ Roger Stern reported.  Shots rang out and Suffolk County Police Lt. Kevin Beyer said three people were hit and others were involved in the fighting.  “We think there was at least five people involved in the fight,” he said.
