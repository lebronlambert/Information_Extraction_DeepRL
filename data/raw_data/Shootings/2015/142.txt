One man is dead and three others injured after a shooting in Taunton on Saturday evening, according to the Bristol County District Attorney's office.  The shooting happened on Weir Street shortly before 6 p.m., according Taunton Police, Massachusetts State Police detectives and Homicide Unit prosecutors, all of whom are actively investigating the incident as a homicide.  Marques McCassie, 24, died from his injuries at Morton Hospital around 6:40 pm.  A suspect has not been identified at this time and a cause for the shooting is unknown.
