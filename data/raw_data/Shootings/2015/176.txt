Shooting outside Miami Gardens night club leaves 1 dead Posted: Tuesday, July 21, 2015 12:08 PM EDT Updated: Tuesday, September 15, 2015 12:08 PM EDT  MIAMI GARDENS, Fla. (WSVN) -- Authorities are investigating after a security guard was fatally shot at a Miami Gardens night club.  The shooting happened outside of Mint Lounge, just after 4 a.m., Tuesday, along Northwest 25th Avenue and Miami Gardens Drive.  According to police, the guard was among four people shot. The three other victims were transported to nearby hospitals.  Police are now looking for the shooter.  If you have any information on this shooting, call Miami-Dade Crime Stoppers at 305-471-TIPS. Remember, you can always remain anonymous, and you may be eligible for a $3,000 reward.
