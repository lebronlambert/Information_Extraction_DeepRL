DETROIT (WWJ) — Detroit police are investigating a shooting that occurred at a block party on Friday night.  Sergeant Cassandra Lewis told WWJ Newsradio 950 that four men were shot — ages 18, 19, 20 and 22 — at a block party on Stronghold Court. All victims were listed in temporary serious conditions after the shooter fired into a crowd of people.  The incident comes one week after the deadly block party shooting on Dexter and Webb that killed one and left 11 others wounded.  Suspects have not been identified.
