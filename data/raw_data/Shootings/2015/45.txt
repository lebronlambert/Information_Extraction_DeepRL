COMPTON (CBSLA.com) — Two female minors and two adult men reportedly were shot at a birthday party in Compton shortly after midnight Sunday.  Around 30 people, including women and children, were at a home in the 2700 block of West Alondra Boulevard when shots rang out around 12:15 a.m., the Los Angeles County Sheriff’s Department reported.  Deputies listed one of the victims as an 8-year-old girl and two of the men were said to be in their 20s.  The four victims were transported to a hospital and all are expected to survive their injuries.  A man was seen fleeing the scene but officials have not made any arrests.  Anyone with information is urged to contact police.
