New Orleans police Tuesday evening (Aug. 25) were investigating the shooting death of a 19-year-old man at the intersection of Olive and Fern streets.  Three other victims arrived at the hospital via private conveyance, police said. They were listed in critical condition. They were hanging out on the corner at the time of the shooting, according to NOPD.  Witnesses told police a black Ford F-150 pickup sped away from the scene of the shooting.  Police have asked anyone with information about the shooting to call Homicide Detective Maggie McCourt at 504.658.5300 or Crimestoppers at 504.822.1111.  In a shooting earlier Tuesday, a man and a toddler were wounded in the 7th Ward.  Stay with NOLA.com | The Times-Picayune as more information becomes available on this breaking story.
