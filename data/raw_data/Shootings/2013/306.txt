Four young people were injured when gunfire broke out at a large house party in Aurora late Saturday, according to Aurora police.  "Three juveniles were hit with small caliber weapon, a fourth was grazed by a bullet," police spokesman Frank Fania said in an email Sunday.  The shooting took place at about 11:40 p.m. in the 2300 block of Paris street. All the victims suffered non life-threatening injuries.  No suspect is in custody.
