At least one dead after shooting near Marshall University Posted: Friday, December 13, 2013 7:09 AM EST  HUNTINGTON (AP)- Police in Huntington are investigating a shooting incident that left several injured and one dead.  The shootings occurred early Friday near the Marshall University campus.  Huntington police confirmed the shootings and the death of one but could not immediately provide additional information.  WOWK-TV reports that a man had been kicked out of a bar for a fight and returned with a gun.  The report says he was killed by a bar worker.
