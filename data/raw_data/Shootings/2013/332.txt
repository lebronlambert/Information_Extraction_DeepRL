LOS ANGELES (CBSLA.com) — The fatal shooting of a man was under investigation in South Los Angeles early Monday morning, the Los Angeles County Sheriff’s Department said.  The shooting occurred around 12:20 a.m. at a party in a warehouse in the 13100 block of Broadway, officials said.  “The suspect vehicle was driving erratically on 132nd Street when the occupant(s) suddenly fired multiple rounds at partygoers standing outside the location,” Deputy Mark Pope said.  An unidentified man was pronounced dead at the scene. Three other males were wounded in the shooting and transported to a local hospital.  “Two of the victims are listed in stable condition, and one victim is in critical condition,” Pope said.  The suspect vehicle was described as a light colored four-door sedan.  Anyone with information about the shooting should contact the Los Angeles County Sheriff’s Department’s Homicide Bureau at (323) 890-5500.
