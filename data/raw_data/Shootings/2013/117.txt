Getty Images Four people were shot in North Long Beach, Calif.  LONG BEACH, Calif. - Police say one person is dead and three others are wounded in a shooting in North Long Beach.  Long Beach Police Department spokeswoman Marlene Arrona says the shooting was reported early Sunday, shortly after midnight.  She says officers arriving to the site on the 300 block of East Home found four victims. All appeared to be adults.  The four were rushed to an area hospital, where one of them died.  No other details of the shooting were immediately available.
