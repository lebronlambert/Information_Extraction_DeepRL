Four people shot in Winton Hills Posted: Monday, May 13, 2013 1:49 PM EDT Updated: Monday, May 13, 2013 3:04 PM EDT  Four people were rushed to area hospitals Monday afternoon after a shooting in Winton Hills.  It happened around 1:30 p.m. in the 100 block of Craft Street right off of Kings Run Drive.      FOX19 has learned that a large group of people were having a confrontation when one man pulled out a gun and began shooting.      Four victims arrived at the hospital via private transportation. The injuries are not believed to be life-threatening.      At this time, police have a vague description of the wanted suspect.
