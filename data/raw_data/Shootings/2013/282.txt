FILED UNDER    BALTIMORE (AP) - One person has been killed and four others were wounded in an early morning shooting in east Baltimore.    Police say officers were called to the 700 block of N. Kenwood Avenue about 1:30 a.m. on Saturday for a report of multiple people shot. Investigators say an unknown number of suspects opened fire on a group of people gathered outside.    Police say 19-year-old Donyae Jones died as a result of her injuries.    Baltimore police have added additional uniformed and plain clothes officers in the area on Saturday.    Investigators are asking any witnesses to come forward with information and contact homicide detectives.
