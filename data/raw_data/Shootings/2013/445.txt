Please enable Javascript to watch this video  ST. LOUIS, MO (KTVI) - A suspect is captured after returning to the scene of a shooting at a St. Louis gas station that left four people injured. Police say it happened a little after 1:30am Sunday morning at a BP station at Compton and Olive. Four men were sitting on the suspect's car and refused to move. After an argument, the suspect got a gun from the car and shot the others.  All the victims, ranging in age from 20 to 34, were taken to a hospital with non-life-threatening injuries.  As police were interviewing witnesses, the 27 year old suspect returned to the scene and was arrested. A weapon was recovered.
