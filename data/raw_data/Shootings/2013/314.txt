CALAIS, Maine (NEWS CENTER) -- According to State Police one person is dead and three others are injured following a shooting.  Police say Daniel Pinney was shot and killed by police after he shot and wounded a woman named Megan Sherrard and her baby at a home on Main Street. Pinney is the baby's father.  A Calais police officer, John Preston, was also wounded. Both Preston and the baby were grazed by bullets and are expected to survive.  Sherrard is in serious condition and was transported by Lifeflight.  The Maine Attorney General's office has sent a team to investigate the shooting.
