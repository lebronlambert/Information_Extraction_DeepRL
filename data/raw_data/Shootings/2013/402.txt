LINDEN – A 35-year-old Linden man was killed Sunday in a shooting in Irvington that also left two others injured, according to an NJ.com report.  Raheem Williams was pronounced dead at University Hospital In Newark about two hours after being shot on the 200 block of Isabella Avenue Sunday evening, according to the report. The Essex County Prosecutor’s Major Crimes Task Force and the Irvington Police Department are investigating the crime.
