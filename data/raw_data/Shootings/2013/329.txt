No arrests have been made, and the investigation is ongoing.  Police are investigating a Saturday night shooting at Marcy Houses that left four people injured and one dead.  According to police, on Saturday, July 6, at around 6:40 p.m., police were called to 472 Marcy Avenue following reports of a shootout.  Police reported four victims of the multiple shooting, including an 18-year-old man identified as Mario Lopez, who died from gunshot wounds to his chest.  The three other victims included a 13-year-old boy who was shot in the neck who reamins in critical condition; and a 14-year-old boy who was shot in the leg and a 15-year-old boy who was shot in the leg, both of whom remain in stable condition.  No arrests have been made.    Anyone with information regarding this incident is asked to call Crime Stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477).
