    FLORENCE, Alabama -- A shooting on West Mobile Street sent four people to area hospitals around 3 a.m. Friday, July 5, according to the Florence Police Department Facebook page.  Chauncey Laray Mitchell, 22, was arrested on suspicion of assault and shooting into an occupied vehicle, according to the police.  Mitchell is being held at the Lauderdale County Detention Center.    Three victims were released from local hospitals early Friday morning. The fourth victim was still being treated for injuries at 8 a.m., according to the police.
