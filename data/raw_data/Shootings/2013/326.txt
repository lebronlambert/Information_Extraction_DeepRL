4 taken to hospital after overnight shooting Posted: Friday, July 5, 2013 9:27 AM EDT Updated: Friday, August 2, 2013 9:28 AM EDT  Four people were transported to the hospital after an early morning shooting in Florence.  Police said they responded to West Mobile Street shortly after 3 a.m. Friday after receiving reports of shots being fired.  Chauncey Laray Mitchell, 22 of Florence, was arrested on assault charges and shooting into an occupied vehicle.  According to authorities, three of the four victims were treated from their injuries and released. The fourth victim is still being treated for his injuries.  Mitchell is being held in the Lauderdale County Detention Center.  Copyright 2013 WAFF. All rights reserved.
