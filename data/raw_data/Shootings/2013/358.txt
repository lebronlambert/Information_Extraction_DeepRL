A man was killed and three others wounded by gunfire late Saturday night outside a northeast Oklahoma City bar, police said.  No names were released Sunday  About 11:20 p.m. Saturday, Oklahoma City police responding to shots fired at 5405 N Lincoln Boulevard found four people with gunshot wounds in the parking lot outside a bar, Sgt. Billy Payne said.  One of them — a man — was pronounced dead at a hospital, he said. The conditions of the other victims were not available.  The shooter has not been arrested, Payne said.
