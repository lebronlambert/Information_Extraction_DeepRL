Boy, 17, slain in East Oakland  A 17-year-old boy was killed and a man was wounded in a shooting in East Oakland, police said Monday.  The victims were standing on the 8600 block of Dowling Street when they were shot about 10 p.m. Sunday.  The teenager, Olajuwon Clayborn of Oakland, died, and the man was taken to Highland Hospital in Oakland with non-life-threatening wounds. His name was not released.  Clayborn was a student at Castlemont High School in Oakland. He previously attended Berkeley High School.  No arrest has been made.
