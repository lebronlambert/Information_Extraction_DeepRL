WINSTON COUNTY, Alabama -- Four people have been found dead in a car in Winston County, according to Sheriff Rick Harris.  Harris said he had not yet been to the scene but the four victims are believed to have suffered gunshot wounds. The car was found on a logging road near a home in a rural area south of Double Springs.  A woman called the sheriff's office around 8 p.m. Wednesday when she saw a suspicious car on the side of the road. Harris said the woman had seen the car as she was going to work Wednesday morning but hadn't thought anything of it.  Forensics units and the Alabama Bureau of Investigation are on the scene, Harris said.  This story will be updated as more information becomes available.
