COLORADO SPRINGS, Colo. -  Five people were injured in a shooting in Colorado Springs early Sunday morning.  Police said officers responded to a hookah bar on the 1600 block of South Nevada Avenue around 12:30 a.m. Sunday.  When they arrived, they found five people who suffered gunshot wounds. Witnesses said they heard dozens of shots fired.  All of the victims were transported to hospitals. None of the injuries are considered life threatening.  Police said this all started between two rival groups. They believe it could be gang related.
