Five people were injured in a shooting outside a Southern Pines billiards bar, according to police.  Police responded to Leather Pockets Billiards and Lounge Sunday around 1:40 a.m. after an officer on patrol reported hearing gunshots.  When police arrived, they found five victims with gunshot wounds in the parking lot of the bar.  No other information was released. Anyone with information is asked to contact the Southern Pines Police Department at (910) 693-1481 or the Crime Tip Line at (910) 693-4110.
