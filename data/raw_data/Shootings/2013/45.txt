suspect-in-bourbon-street-shooting2.jpg (Photo: WWL)  WWLTV.com  Email: webteam@wwltv.com | Twitter: @WWLTV  NEW ORLEANS New Orleans police have released video of a suspect in a February quadruple shooting on Bourbon Street.  The shooting happened Feb. 9 in the 400 block of Bourbon Street.  NOPD spokesman Officer Frank Robertson said the suspect goes by the name of Brandon, B Money and B. He may be driving a 2006 four-door silver or gray Chevrolet Impala.  He 'may have critical information' about the shooting, Robertson said.  Robertson said the suspect may reside on the West Bank of Jefferson Parish, either in Marrero or Harvey.  Three other suspects have already been arrested in the case.
