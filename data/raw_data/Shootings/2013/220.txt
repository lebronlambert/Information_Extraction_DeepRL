Person of interest named in Flint quadruple shooting Posted: Saturday, May 25, 2013 6:44 PM EDT Updated: Sunday, June 23, 2013 11:26 AM EDT  Flint police have named Antonio King Green as a person of interest in a shooting that left one person dead and three others in the hospital.  Officials were called to 1101 W. Paterson St., near Proctor Ave. in Flint around 2:15 p.m.  Lt. Marcus Mahan said a group of women were seen fighting in the area shortly before two men hopped out of a car and opened fire.  Flint police said four people were shot, including three women.  Mahan said one woman was shot in the head and another was shot in the abdomen.  All four victims were taken to Hurley Medical Center where two were listed in critical condition and the other two victims were in serious condition.  One of the victims later died.  If you have any information about Green's whereabouts, you're asked to call Flint police at 810-237-6800.  Copyright 2013 WNEM (Meredith Corporation). All rights reserved.
