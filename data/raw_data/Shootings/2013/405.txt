The Yakima County sheriff’s office says four teenagers were shot and wounded as they left a party at Granger.  The sheriff’s office says they were approaching their car about 3 a.m. Sunday when they were confronted by a man and exchanged gang slurs. The man pulled a pistol, fired several shots and fled.  The victims were treated at a hospital and all are expected to survive. All four are from Mattawa and are 14, 16, 17 and 19 years old.
