(WXYZ) - Police say four people were shot in Inkster Thursday evening.  Officers say they were enroute to a shooting on Oakwood when they heard that an individual showed up at a nearby hospital with a gunshot wound.  When they arrived to the scene, officers found two victims who had also been shot, according to police. While at the scene, they heard of another person being admitted to another hospital with gunshot wounds.  Police say this is an ongoing investigation and they still aren't able to confirm if all of the victims were involved in a single incident.  The two victims found on Oakwood have non-life threatening injuries, according to police.  Police are searching for a male suspect who was driving a black Dodge Charger.
