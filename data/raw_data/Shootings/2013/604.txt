CANTON, Ohio - One person has been arrested for a shooting in a Canton bar parking lot early Sunday morning.  Police say they responded to the Whistle Stop Bar on W. Tuscarawas Street after 2:30am to find four people had been shot.  Juantina Despasquale, 45, Amanda Depasquale, 27, and Vanessa Van Almen, 24, were all taken to area hospitals.  Terrell Lamar Skinner, 26, was pronounced dead at the scene by the Stark County Coroner's Office.  The suspect, Dwight Ellis, was still at the scene when officers arrived and was arrested.  He was charged with aggravated murder and three counts of felonious assault.
