WASHINGTON— District of Columbia police are searching for a suspect who opened fire at a northeast Washington nightclub, sending five people to the hospital.  Police say it happened around 2 a.m. Saturday at the D.C. Soundstage nightclub on Benning Road. Police say all five people who were shot are expected to survive.
