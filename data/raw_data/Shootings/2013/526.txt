A shooting at the Plush nightclub in Jacksonville Friday morning put four people in the hospital, including one in life-threatening condition, according to police.  Off-duty officers working outside the 845 University Blvd. N club found three people shot after a shooting inside caused a mass exodus about 2:15 a.m., said Sgt. Steve Zona.  A fourth person drove to the hospital with a cut wound and is in stable condition, Zona said.
