Winchester police say one person has been killed, and three others have been injured in a shooting at an apartment complex Tuesday night.    Emergency crews were called to the Summit Apartments on Oxford Drive just after 8 p.m.  Winchester police say the three people injured have been taken to UK Hospital for treatment. One of them has life-threatening injuries.    Police say they found the victims on two different floors of an apartment building. At this time, they do not think this shooting was random.    Police say they are looking for suspects in this case, but they haven't released any information about them.
