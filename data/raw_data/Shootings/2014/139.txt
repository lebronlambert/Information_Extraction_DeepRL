A 15-year-old boy was killed and seven people were injured in a shooting late Friday night at a graduation party in Gentilly, New Orleans police said.  Watch report  The boy, four women and three men were injured around 11:20 p.m. in the 6200 block of Eads Street when at least one person fired a gun during an argument at the party, police said. The names of the victims were not released.  Four women, in ages ranging from 19 to 30, were injured. Three suffered non-life-threatening injuries. One woman needed surgery, police said. Three men, in ages ranging from 22 to 25, suffered non-life-threatening injuries.  The 15-year-old boy suffered a gunshot wound and died at a hospital, police said.  Detectives are investigating the incident to determine the motive and identify suspects.  Anyone with information on a crime can call Crimestoppers at 504-822-1111.
