A shooting at a Graniteville night club early Saturday morning left one man dead and three injured.  Stacy M. Johnson, 20, of Wagener, was pronounced dead at the scene of the shooting, which occurred at Round Two Bar and Grill at about 1:25 a.m., according to Aiken County Coroner Tim Carlton.  Aiken County Sheriffs Office deputies responded to the bar, located at 192 Breezy Hill Road, after receiving multiple calls about a large fight that resulted in shots fired, according to a press release.  Deputies found Johnson dead at the scene, and three others suffering from gunshot wounds, police said.  The three other victims were taken to the hospital.  Two of the victims are being treated for non-life-threatening injuries, and the third victims condition was unknown, according to police.  Johnson had allegedly gone to the bar with friends after attending a football game in Saluda, Carlton said. Strom Thurmond High School played against Saluda Friday night.  An autopsy is scheduled today in Newberry.  A suspect has not yet been identified to the media and police are investigating the shooting.  Anyone with information about the shooting is asked to call 803-648-6811 or Crimestopers at 1-888-Crime-SC.
