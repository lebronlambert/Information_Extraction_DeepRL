(WMC) - Seven people were shot outside of a Memphis nightclub early Sunday morning.        Police say the victims were shot at Club 108 at 2517 Shasta, near North Hollywood Street, just after 2 a.m.        All seven were taken to the hospital, six in non-critical and one in critical condition.      No arrests have been made and police are investigating the incident.
