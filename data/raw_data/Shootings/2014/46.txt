The New Orleans Police Department is investigating the shooting of four people at a popular New Orleans East nightclub Thursday night.  The shooting happened around midnight, at Club H2O, in the 4600 block of Downman Road.  The shooting victims include three men and one woman, and police say all four victims were taken to the hospital with what appears to be non-life threatening injuries.
