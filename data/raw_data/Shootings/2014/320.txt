SPRINGFIELD, Mo. -  Springfield police say three people died after a shooting early Saturday at a northside motel.  Lt. Culley Wilson says police responded at about 7:45 a.m. Saturday after receiving a call to check on a man on the floor in one of the rooms at the Economy Inn at 2555 N. Glenstone Ave. Officers found three people dead in the room. All three were shot to death. At a late afternoon news briefing, Wilson would not release the names, ages or genders of the victims.  A fourth person was taken to a hospital for treatment of a gunshot wound. Late Saturday afternoon, Wilson said the unidentified man was in stable condition.  Wilson says investigators do not yet have a suspect or a motive for the killings. Wilson added that they have several witnesses who are cooperating with detectives.
