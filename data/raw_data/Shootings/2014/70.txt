WILMINGTON (CBSLA.com) — Four people reportedly were shot early Saturday at a family party in South LA.  LAPD Sgt. Ed Piago says gunfire was reported at 2:45 a.m. in the 1200 block of West Papeete Street, located in Wilmington.  Around 40 people were celebrating an 18-year-old’s birthday in the backyard.  Several people were standing in front of the home when someone drove by and opened fire, Piago said.  Three men and a woman were injured. One person was transported to a hospital with critical injuries and three others were treated for non-life threatening wounds.  Officers at the scene say the suspect drove away in a dark-colored BMW.  Detectives asked anyone with information about the attack to call them at (310) 726-7900.
