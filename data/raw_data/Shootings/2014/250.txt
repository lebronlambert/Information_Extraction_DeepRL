Four people were wounded, two critically, in a drive-by shooting in Queens early Sunday morning.Police say just before 6 a.m., two men driving a silver Audi exited the vehicle and opened fire in front of 153-38 118th Avenue in South Jamaica.The gunmen took off and were last seen heading westbound on 118th Avenue.All of the victims were taken to Jamaica Hospital.A 27-year old man is in critical condition with gunshot wounds to the chest and back, and a 26-year old man is also critical after being shot in the chest.Another man, 19, is in stable condition with a gunshot wound in the hand, and a woman, 23, is in stable condition after being shot in the hip.
