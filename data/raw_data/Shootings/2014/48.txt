Oakland shooting kills 1, wounds 3 in Fruitvale  A 36-year-old man was killed and three others were wounded Wednesday night in a shooting in Oakland's Fruitvale neighborhood, police said.  The victims, two women and two men, were shot on 37th Avenue near International Boulevard about 9:50 p.m. One of the men died at a hospital, and the others, a 24-year-old man and two women, ages 18 and 26, are expected to survive.  The names of the victims weren't released. Police have not reported an arrest.
