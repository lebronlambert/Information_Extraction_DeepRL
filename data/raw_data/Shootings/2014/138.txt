One man was killed and three others were hospitalized on Friday night following a quadruple shooting in Bellflower, authorities said.  The shooting occurred in the 9500 block of Flower Street (map), according to a Los Angeles County Sheriff’s Department news release.  Three men were taken to a local hospital, according to the release Their conditions were not immediately known.  The victim who was fatally shot was described only as an adult male.  Homicide detectives were on the way to investigate, the release stated.  No further information was immediately available.  KTLA’s Nidia Becerra Tijerino contributed to this report.
