Update: One person in custody after early morning shooting leaves five people injured  FLINT, MI -- Five people were shot outside a party store in Flint around 3 a.m. Thursday morning, police say.  The shooting happened on Martin Luther King Avenue near West Dayton Street in Flint, sending five people to the hospital where four are listed in good condition and one is in serious condition, said Lt. Brian Cole with the Michigan State Police.  Investigators are interviewing witnesses, and the investigation is ongoing, Cole said.  No further information has been released.
