Four teens were sent to the hospital after a fight led to a shooting.  The shooting happened around 10:30 p.m. Friday.  Police said two groups of teens got into a fight at The After Dark Lounge on Parsons Avenue.  The party was shut down and the groups went their separate ways.  As one group walked away, a car pulled up and opened fire. Members of the walking group returned fire.  Two teen girls and two teen boys, ranging between 16 and 17-years-old, were injured during the exchange of gunfire.  None of the victims' injuries are said to be life-threatening.
