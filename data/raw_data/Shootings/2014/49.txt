At least one of the four people shot at a Stockton park Wednesday has died, police said.  The shooting took place at Constitution Park on Fremont Street near Pilgrim Street at about 4:30 p.m.  Watch report: 1 dead, 3 injured in Stockton shooting  Police said a 20-year-old man is dead, and the other three victims are a 17-year-old boy, an 18-year-old man and a 22-year-old man.  Three other people who were shot were taken to a hospital, but there is no word on their condition.  Officers said this is a high crime neighborhood, and there has been gang activity in the area before.    Police are talking to neighbors to try and find any witnesses to the shooting.  So far, no arrests have been made. Police do not have a motive or a description of the suspect.  Stay with KCRA 3 News for more on this developing story.
