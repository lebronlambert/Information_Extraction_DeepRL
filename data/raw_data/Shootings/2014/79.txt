Man killed, 3 hurt in North Richmond shooting  A man was killed and three others were hurt in a shooting in North Richmond, authorities said Sunday.  The shooting happened in front of a home on the 1600 block of First Street about 10 p.m. Saturday, said Jimmy Lee, spokesman for the Contra Costa County Sheriff's Office, which patrols the unincorporated community.  Deputies arrived and found two victims at the scene. One, a 23-year-old man, was taken by helicopter to John Muir Medical Center in Walnut Creek, where he died.  Three other people, including two who arrived by private vehicle, suffered non-life-threatening wounds and were being treated at Doctors Medical Center in San Pablo.  No arrests have been made.
