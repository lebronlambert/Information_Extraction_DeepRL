DETROIT (WXYZ) - Detroit Police tell 7 Action News four people were shot outside of a club late Friday night, not 7 people as originally reported.  Detroit Police say the shooting happened around 11 p.m. last night, during skirmishes and fights outside of the club. According to police, a dark colored vehicle, possibly a Buick Rendezvous or Pontiac Aztec, driving very slowly, opened fire.  Four males, ages 15, 17, and two 20 year olds were shot. They were taken to the hospital and are currently in temporary serious condition.  Detroit police are asking anyone with information to the incident to call them at 313-596-5940, or Crime Stoppers at 1-800-speakup.
