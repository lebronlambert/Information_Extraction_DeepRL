CLEVELAND, Ohio -- The Northern Ohio Violent Fugitive Task Force is offering a reward for tips that lead to the capture of a Cleveland man accused of attempted murder.  Wesley Daley Jr., 27, is sought by the U.S. Marshals Service, the Cuyahoga County Sheriff's Department and the Lakewood Police Department. Authorities said Daley shot at moving vehicles twice in June. Daley struck four people, including a 7-year-old girl, during the incidents, according to the task force.  Daley's last known address is on the 4000 block of Cypress Avenue. Authorities believe he is hiding on the city's West Side.  Anyone with information about Daley is asked to call the Northern Ohio Violent Fugitive Task Force at 1-866-4WANTED. Tips also can be sent via text message with the keyword "WANTED" to 847411.
