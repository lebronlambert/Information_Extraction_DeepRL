4 men hospitalized and arrested after Atlanta shooting Posted: Sunday, January 5, 2014 8:34 AM EST Updated: Sunday, February 2, 2014 8:34 AM EST  Four men were hospitalized and eventually arrested after a shooting in Atlanta on Saturday.  According to Atlanta police, officers responded to 2025 Peachtree Road around noon Saturday, responding to a call of a person shot.  When officers arrived, they say three men were found with gunshot wounds. All three were taken to Grady Memorial Hospital.  Authorities said another man was found at Atlanta Medical Center, also with a gunshot wound from the same incident.  According to police, the shooting was connected to illegal drugs.  Police say all four men were in stable condition Saturday and taken into police custody at the hospital.  Copyright 2014 WGCL-TV (Meredith Corporation). All rights reserved.
