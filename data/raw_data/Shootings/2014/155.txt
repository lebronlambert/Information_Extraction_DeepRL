A man is in police custody after leading them on an overnight high speed pursuit.  Metro Police were called to an attempted burglary on the 3100 block of Sirius Ave. The suspect would not get out of his vehicle after police asked him to come out. The man then rammed his vehicle into Metro's vehicles before leading them on a chase.  Police pulled him over eventually near Desert Inn and Arville.  The suspect was arrested on several charges, including starting a fire near the scene of the robbery.
