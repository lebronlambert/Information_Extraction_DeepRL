ST. LOUIS (KMOX) – A witness told St. Louis city police a shootout near the corner of Natural Bridge and Marcus, around 3:30 Sunday morning, left four men injured.  The victims told investigators they were leaving a club when two persons fired shots at them.  The injured range in age from 27 to 37.  Three are in stable condition with gunshot wounds.
