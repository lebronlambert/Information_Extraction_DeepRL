A Saturday night shooting in Belle Glade left four people hospitalized, according to a Palm Beach County Sheriff's Office news release.  It happened about 8:20 p.m. as a group of people were standing outside in the 600 block of Covenant Drive. That's when a vehicle drove by and opened fire, leaving four people hit.  Palm Beach County Fire Rescue took the victims to a local hospital.  Investigators set up a perimeter after more than one person ran toward a nearby cane field, according to the release.  Deputies also found a vehicle riddled with bullets, but it is not known if the vehicle is related to the shooting. The investigation remains underway.
