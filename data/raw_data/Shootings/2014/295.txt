One man is dead and three others were wounded after a shooting in the city's West Side, police said.The four men were in a vehicle in the 4400-block of West Van Buren in West Garfield Park when they were shot at around 3 a.m. Saturday, police said.Officials say a 20-year-old man died at the hospital, and two 23-year-olds and a 24-year-old are in the hospital with injuries.
