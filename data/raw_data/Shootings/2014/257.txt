× Four dead, including two children, in Oklahoma apartment shooting  TULSA , Okla. – Four people are dead, including two young children after a shooting in an east Tulsa apartment complex.  Tulsa Police say they found four people who were shot to death around 12:30 a.m. Tuesday at the Bradford Townhouse Apartments.  According to Tulsa World, an adult man, 35, and woman, 22, were found, along with a 3-year-old boy and 5-year-old girl.  Investigators say a toddler was found alive inside the apartment sitting on the couch.  The shootings are still under investigation, but police say it could be a murder-suicide.
