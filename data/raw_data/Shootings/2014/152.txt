OAK PARK (WWJ) – One person is dead and others injured after shootings in Oak Park on Saturday.  Oak Park police confirm that one man is dead and three others injured after gunfire erupted Saturday afternoon at the Village Green Condos on Rensselaer , near Ten Mile and Coolidge roads.  Police say two men and one woman were taken to the hospital with gunshot wounds.  Two people have been arrested. Police say they do not believe it’s gang related.  Meanwhile, a resident tells WWJ they believe it was a domestic dispute.  The investigation is ongoing.
