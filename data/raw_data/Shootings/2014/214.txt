Four Shot, Three Dead in Dallas Shooting  Police are investigating after four people were shot Saturday morning at a home on Wynell Street in Dallas, just south of the Dallas VA Medical Center. (Published Saturday, Aug. 2, 2014)  Police are investigating after four people were shot Saturday morning at a home on Wynell Street in Dallas, just south of the Dallas VA Medical Center.  Three people were found deceased at the scene. The fourth person remains alive at Baylor.  Medical examiners arrived at the scene around 8 a.m. They are still trying to piece together what happened.  Senior Cpl. DeMarquis Black said that a gunman was seen fleeing, but had no information about the suspect.  Black said police believe the area where the shooting occurred was a "point of sale" for drugs.  Police have not publicly identified the victims.
