BAKERSFIELD, Calif. - The Kern County Sheriff's Office said three people were shot and killed inside a residence in the 1700 block of Quincy Street late Wednesday night.  The Sheriff's Office released the names of the victims early Friday morning.  Zephaniah Lee Matthews, 29, Junior Alfonzo Contreras, 23, and Daniel Terelle Watkins, 22 died after being shot by another person.  A fourth person injured in the shooting is in stable condition at a Bakersfield-area hospital, according to KCSO Lt. Dean Marshall.  Lt. Marshall also told 23ABC there is no information yet on any suspects.
