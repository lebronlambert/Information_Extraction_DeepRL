4 injured in party bus shooting (Photo: KARE)  MINNEAPOLIS - Four people are injured after a shooting involving a party bus in downtown Minneapolis.  Police say officers were called to a shooting at about 2:30 a.m. Saturday near 3rd Street North and Hennepin Avenue.  Near 3rd Street and Nicollet Avenue, they found three adults who had gunshot wounds that were not life-threatening.  Police spokesman John Elder tells the St. Paul Pioneer Press that all victims were "related or associated" with a party bus. They were taken to Hennepin County Medical Center by ambulance.  About an hour later, a fourth victim from the same incident arrived at the hospital.  The victims have not been identified.  Elders says the shooting suspect left the scene and there have been no arrests.  Copyright 2014 The Associated Press. All rights reserved.
